# Rin Satsuki

This mod adds a new lightweight QoL service in the form of the forgotten (scrapped) heroine from EoSD: Rin Satsuki!

Long ago, there existed a mod not too different from this one, under RinSatsukiMod.
However, since no one has been able to contact the developer about porting their mod in nearly 6 years at the time of writing; I have taken the opportunity to create it from the ground up with my own take/vision of the character.

Admittedly, I am not the best writer, and I am working off bits and pieces of a scrapped character's lore, so personality/quirks is heavily reliant on assumptions and media literacy.
I will try to keep it as close to the source material as possible, but if you have any suggestions, let me know on the mod's GitHub page! Bug reports, feedback, additions are all greatly appreciated. Moreover, this is my first published mod, so code optimizations via PRs are also sweet.

---

## As for what the NPC actually does in the current state of the mod (0.1):

* Moves in with no pre-requisites, you can have Rin Satsuki at the start of the game!
* Provides quick renewable access to Regular Mushrooms, Glowing Mushrooms, Pumpkin Seeds, and Sunflowers.
* Sells Daybloom, Blinkroot, Waterleaf, and Shiverthorn at the Pre-Boss tier.
* Sells Deathweed and the Evil Mushrooms at the Post-Evil Boss tier.
* Sells Moonglow and Fireblossom at the Post-Skeletron tier.
* Sells all moss types at the Post-WoF tier.
* Sells Life Fruit at the Post-Plantera tier.
* And finally, she even has affordable healthcare! (Don't tell the U.S.)

Aside from the lightweight nature of the mod, Rin Satsuki's prices are fairly lenient, and even more so when tailoring to max happiness. Progression is kept in mind as possible too, so the mod does not feel too heavily cheaty, yet not so pointless or redundant.
